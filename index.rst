Introduction to Rackspace Cloud
===============================

Introduction
------------

This guide was designed to serve as an introduction to the Rackspace Public
Cloud. It covers core concepts which are important to understand before
interacting with the Rackspace Cloud, from signing up for an account to methods
for interacting with your account and how to request support.

**This guide is currently a work in progress.** Issues regarding the contents
can be submitted to
https://github.com/rackerlabs/docs-intro-to-rackspace-cloud/issues.
